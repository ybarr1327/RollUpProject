Kids


Allowing your kids to practice Brazilian Jiu Jitsu is one of the greatest investments you will ever make. It will teach them valuable skills that will stick with them for life. Skills such as self discipline, confidence, self defense, confidence, body awareness, and to be great movers. Brazilian jiu jitsu will teach your kids all of these things.





Adults


Jiu jitsu gives you the confidence and structure to build up your self-esteem. As you progress through each training level, you will find yourself gaining respect for your abilities and your journey. Confidence is critical to mastering a martial art, which is why our instructors guide you every step of the way, helping you perfect techniques until you can comfortably perform them with little forethought. Adults jiu jitsu lessons in Brazilian jiu jitsu has the power to change your life. Through increased focus and diligent practice, you will find yourself in better shape with less stress and more confidence than before. .





Open Mat


Open mat is a designated time for you and your teammates to use the mats as you’d like. There’s no actual class at these times, so it’s up to you to decide what you use it for. You and your training partners are left to your own devices, and you can use this time for lots of things.
